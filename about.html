About Michy R. Photography


Michy R. Photography is a professional non-traditional portrait photographer in Miami, Broward Florida specializing in family photography, children photography, baby photography, maternity photography. Michy R. is so blessed to be able to use her artistic background to combine baby portraits, children portraits, and family portraits and create beautiful images to preserve the memories of your portrait sessions and events.


If you are looking for a creative and talented family photographer, children photographer, baby photographer and maternity photographer then you have no need to look any further. Michy R photographs in the Dade and Broward areas.
